How Our Car Title Loan Process Works

We have made it really easy for you to get a title loan! Our process is simple and fast, so that you have a great experience with Imperial Valley Loan Center. The whole process is only 3 steps and we can have money in your hands as fast as an hour from now! A car title loan is easy to qualify for because you are present value of the car to secure a loan today. You are not selling the car to us. You are just using the vehicle as a collateral to get a loan.

Step One: Apply Online

Fill out the short form. As soon as you do this, we can make a decision on how much we can lend you today. Don't worry if you have bad credit or no credit history. Since the vehicle is used as a security, we can offer loans to almost anybody, even if you have bad credit.

Step Two: Quick Information

You have to send us some basic information like your Driver's License. You can either fax or email these documents to us. The list is short and very simple.

Step Three: Getting Your Money!

When you come to our office, you will be greeted by a professional auto title loan processor. They will be ready and waiting to assist you in finalizing the loan. The sales staff will take a look at your vehicle, explain your loan documents, and hand you a check. The whole process can take less than 10 minutes, after which you have your money.

If you are unable to come to our office, don't worry, we'll come to you!! Once again one of our professional customer service representatives will be there for you. They will do everything within their power to help provide you with the money you need.
